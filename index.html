Home Health Care

Home health care is when medical professionals provide care in a person's own home. This can include nursing care, therapy, and assistance with daily activities like bathing and eating. It's often used for seniors or those recovering from surgery or illness. It helps people stay comfortable and independent in familiar surroundings.

Pediatric Care

Pediatric care is medical care specifically for children, from infants to teenagers. It focuses on their unique health needs, growth, and development. Pediatric care includes check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for childhood illnesses and injuries. It ensures children grow up healthy and happy.
